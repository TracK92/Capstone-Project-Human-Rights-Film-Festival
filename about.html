ECHO INTERNATIONAL

HUMAN RIGHTS FILM FESTIVAL 2022

Echo International Human Rights Film Festival is an event that is primarily geared towards the celebration of human rights through film. The 2022 festival aims to attract people globally.

Film is an effective and impactul ways of communicating a message as important as "human rights matter for all" in contemporary society.

We have invited filmmakers with a keen interest in various aspects of human rights from all over the world. Therefore, if you're a filmmaker, a human rights enthusiast or just an interested party, we hope to see you there. Let's stand together for all humanity.

Please contact us via Email for any further questions concerning Echo International Human Rights Festival 2022!
